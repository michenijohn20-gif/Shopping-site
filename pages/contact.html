Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?+
Browse the catalog, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Pay via M-Pesa, card, or choose pay-on-delivery.
How fast is delivery?+
Next-day in Nairobi for orders placed before midnight. Upcountry deliveries take 1–3 business days.
Are all products genuine?+
Yes. Every product is 100% genuine with official receipt and manufacturer warranty. No fakes, ever.
What payment methods do you accept?+
M-Pesa (Till & Paybill), Visa, Mastercard, bank transfer, and cash on delivery.
How do returns work?+
Return within 7 days in original condition for a full refund. Contact us to initiate the return.
Do you deliver outside Nairobi?+
Yes! We deliver across all 47 counties. Contact us for delivery quotes to your specific town.
